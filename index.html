Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 18, 2026

Thank you for using Truth or Dare ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account and does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

Analytics & Data Collection

We use third-party analytics services to better understand how users interact with our application on Android devices and to continuously improve the app experience.

Google Firebase Analytics

For users running the Android variant of our application, we utilize Google Firebase Analytics, a web analysis service provided by Google LLC ("Google"). Firebase Analytics automatically collects and processes certain diagnostic and usage data.

Types of Data Collected Automatically:

  • Device Identifiers: Mobile device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID (AAID) and unique Firebase installation IDs.
  • Usage Metrics: Information regarding app interactions, such as screen views, session duration, app launches, and user engagement flows.
  • Technical & Diagnostics Data: Approximate geographic location (derived from IP addresses), device model, operating system version, and system performance details.

Purpose of Data Processing:

This data is processed solely for internal application optimization, performance monitoring, and analytics purposes. We do not use this data for personalized ad targeting, nor do we sell, trade, or rent this information to third-party ad networks.

Data Protection & Sharing:

All data captured via the Firebase SDK is encrypted in transit using secure HTTPS/TLS connections. Google processes this data as a service provider on our behalf in accordance with their privacy guidelines. You can learn more about how Google handles data by visiting the Google Privacy & Terms site.

User Content and Local Storage

The App may allow users to create, save, or customize content, including custom Truth or Dare questions.

Any content created or added by users is stored locally on the user's device and is not uploaded to our servers. We do not collect, review, store, or share user-created questions or other locally stored content.

Downloaded Game Content

From time to time, the App may download additional Truth and Dare questions, game packs, or content updates to enhance gameplay.

All downloaded content is created, reviewed, maintained, and distributed exclusively by the App's developers.

No downloadable game content is contributed by users or other third parties. Downloaded content is provided solely to improve the gameplay experience and does not contain user-generated submissions.

Advertising

The App may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising networks such as Google AdMob.

These providers may automatically collect information including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers
  • IP address
  • Language settings
  • App interaction data
  • Diagnostic information

This information may be used to deliver, measure, and improve advertising services.

Learn more about Google's privacy practices:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics and Diagnostics

The App may use analytics and crash reporting tools to understand usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve performance.

Information collected may include device information, application version, crash reports, and general usage statistics.
